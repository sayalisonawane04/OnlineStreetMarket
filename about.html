Join Us

Join as a Vendor

Are you a local artisan or vendor? Follow these steps to showcase your products and reach more customers!

Step 1: Sign Up

Fill out the registration form to create your vendor account.

Step 2: Add Your Products

Upload images and details of your products for customers to view.

Step 3: Set Your Prices

Decide on pricing for each product and any discounts you want to offer.

Step 4: Start Selling

Once approved, your products will be visible to customers. Start selling!

Join as a Customer

Register today to start shopping from local vendors and discover unique products! Follow these steps:

Step 1: Create an Account

Sign up by filling in your details to create a customer account.

Step 2: Browse Products

Explore various categories and find products that interest you.

Step 3: Add to Cart

Select your favorite items and add them to your shopping cart.

Step 4: Checkout

Complete your purchase by filling out your shipping information.
